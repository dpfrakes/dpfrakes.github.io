October 15, 2022

What if software engineering jobs could be accomplished without a screen? What would that look like? Increased screen time deteriorates eye health, attention span, and capacity for imagination and creativity. Many software engineers I know, particularly experienced ones like myself, cherish their time offline, even if they still enjoy the job of solving problems that software engineers do.
